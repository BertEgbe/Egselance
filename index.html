What do we have in store?

We provide a safe and conducive environment for anyone looking to get in shape, stay fit and acquaint themselves with a personal trainer to help them attain those workout goals.

Where can you find us?

Our workout studios are located across all the major cities in the Netherlands and we are working on expanding that outreach to the other 2 BENELUX countries.

Why train with us?

Our training equipment are modern, efficient and of high-quality. We also have large workout spaces which makes it all the more effective for our members to be socially distanced from each other as we continue to curb the spread of the coronavirus.

How do we ensure safety?

To establish a safe workout space for everyone coming to any of our studios, we've increased the frequency at which equipment are cleanup, decommission some to ensure safe distance between people, limit the number of people permitted to use TheSweatSpace at every hour.
